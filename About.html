    Our journey began with one humble shop in Baburhat, Coochbehar and grew to over one thousand locations throughout India under the inspiring vision of our founder, Ratilal Datta in the early 1999. Since then, our family has combined their years of experience with the vision of a community of collaborative shop owners, suppliers and other partners working together to create a brand name in the market to grow their business to what it is today
